EasyCall Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Information We Collect

EasyCall does not collect, store, or share any personal data. The application has no proprietary servers and does not send information to third parties.

Permissions Used

The application requests the following permissions that are used exclusively on your device:

  • Contacts: To display photos and phone numbers of your contacts. This information never leaves your device.
  • Phone: To make calls and attempt to activate hands-free mode.
  • Accessibility (optional): To automatically activate hands-free mode on some devices.

Data Storage

All data (configuration of selected contacts) is stored only on your device using SharedPreferences. No data is transmitted to any external server.
